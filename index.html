Swartz IV

A long time ago

The virus that must not be named had destroyed the civilization as we knew before 2021. In order to recreate our world, web developpers were called to help.
As they were not many up to that year, BeCode was charged to give the looked forward skills to 30+ people.
BeCode gave them the force. Now, it was up to them whether they were going to use those competences for the greater good, or go to the dark side and help the humanity vanish for good.
